Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients: Spaghetti, minced meat, tomatoes, garlic, onion

Steps:

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente, then drain.
  2. Sauté chopped onion and garlic in a pan until soft.
  3. Add minced meat and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in chopped tomatoes and simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  5. Mix the sauce with the spaghetti.
  6. Serve hot with grated cheese if desired.

Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients: Flour, eggs, milk, sugar, baking powder

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. In another bowl, beat the eggs and mix with milk.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed.
  4. Heat a lightly greased skillet over medium heat.
  5. Pour batter onto the skillet (about 1/4 cup per pancake).
  6. Flip when bubbles appear; cook until golden on both sides.
  7. Serve with syrup, butter, or fruits.

Garden Salad

Ingredients: Lettuce, tomato, cucumber, olives, vinaigrette

Steps:

  1. Wash all vegetables thoroughly.
  2. Chop lettuce, tomato, and cucumber into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Place all vegetables in a large bowl and add olives.
  4. Drizzle with vinaigrette dressing.
  5. Toss gently to combine.
  6. Serve chilled as a side or main dish.
